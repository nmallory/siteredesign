ABOUT

Maggie Cocco is an American composer, performer, and educator based in New Zealand. She has composed and/or performed for various music projects, including but not limited to Maggie Cocco’s Science for Sociopaths, The Maggie Cocco Band, The San Detroit Band, The Lucky Devils, Hella Fitzgerald, and Pure Ecstasy.

Ms. Cocco teaches and applies her skills to various live and studio collaborations at home and abroad.

Primary genres: Neomellow, Soft Rock, Blues Rock, Americana, R&B

Who is Maggie Cocco?

Maggie Cocco is a Southern born, Detroit raised, San Francisco based singer-songwriter. Fans of singer-songwriters from the 60s to today will love her “sincere and timeless style". She has performed and released her original material under the moniker "Science for Sociopaths" for audiences worldwide since 2015. Ms. Cocco divides her time between teaching and applying her talents to various live and studio collaborations at home and abroad.

Sounds Like: Sheryl Crowe, Melissa Etheridge, Carole King, Joni Mitchell
